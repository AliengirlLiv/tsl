Break-Ins Across the 5Cs

Zaila Argent | Dec. 13, 2019, 11:26 p.m.

Tuesday at approximately 1400, Campus Security notified the Claremont student body about a series of break-ins occurring around the 5C’s. Among the unfortunate victims mentioned included the CMC chemistry department, the Pomona woodshop, Green Bikes, and the Oldenborg dorm belonging to the freshman that posted about his lost lanyard on 5C for Sale/For Free. In short, the alleged burglars knew to hit all the locations on campus that no one cares about #screwpomona.

What CampSec’s descriptions lacked, however, was a list of what these thieves could have possibly taken from all of these targets, as they are infamous for their valuable possessions. The Golden Antlers have put on their detectives hat, bought a crate of magnifying glasses, and gotten to work to deliver you answers. This is what we found:

Chemistry department:
40% ethanol
Collection of tears from organic chemistry students
Skeleton
Canned brain

Woodshop:
A replica of CMC’s phallic art installation
Wood ;) ;)

Green Bikes; everything they own
A broken chain
Flat tire
Mousetrap

Oldenburg Dorm
Harvard: Pomona of the East t-shirt
A copy of Infinite Jest
